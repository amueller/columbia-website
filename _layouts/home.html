Enrolling in COMS W4995 Applied Machine Learning Spring 2020

Applied Machine learning will be held again in Spring 2020, MS DS students can enroll in COMS W4995 via SSOL. External students have to follow the Cross-Registration Instructions outlined by DSI. More details on the course website.

You can find the materials from last year on the previous course website, and videos from last year on youtube. The content in 2020 will closely resemble the previous content.

Auditing Applied Machine Learning

If you are not able to enroll in the course or would like to audit for other reasons, anyone interested will be added to the coursework and piazza platforms. Please email a CA to get yourself added. If you are auditing, your exams and homework will not be graded.
